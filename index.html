Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is NeuroPlan really free?

Yes! 100% free forever. No premium plans, no hidden costs, no subscriptions. All features are available to everyone.

Do I need to create an account?

No! The planner works entirely in your browser. No registration, no login, no personal information required.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Once you download the files, it works completely offline. Perfect for studying anywhere without internet.

Will my data be saved?

Absolutely! All your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage and persists across sessions.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes! The planner is fully responsive and works great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

How do I backup my data?

Use the built-in export feature to download your data as a JSON file. You can import it anytime to restore.
